After Step 4

What happens next?

SUBMIT  →  QUEUED  →  ADMIN REVIEWS  →  EMAIL SENT  →  SIGN IN
  • Application is queued for review by an admin.
  • You receive an email when approved (or rejected, with reason).
  • Approval time depends on admin workload.
  • Do not register again while waiting — duplicates are rejected automatically.

Section 7

Common Issues

"Email already exists"

You already have an account or a pending application. Try Sign In, or use forgot password.

"Username not available"

Pick another. Usernames are unique across the entire portal.

"Use format: 09XXXXXXXXX"

Philippine mobile only — exactly 11 digits, no spaces or dashes.

Upload fails

File too large (try under 5MB), wrong format (use JPG/PNG), or slow connection.

"Pending Review" never changes

Approval is manual. Wait for email — if longer than a few days, contact the admin.

"Location needed for ETA"

Browser blocked location. Allow location for the site, then click Refresh location.

Form lost after refresh

Step data is not preserved across page reloads. Use Back within the form instead.

Sign In fails with correct password

Caps Lock? Try email vs username. Account may not be approved yet.

Need help?

Three ways to get assistance with your registration or portal account.

Admin Support

Contact the portal admin for in-person help with registration, approval status, or password resets.

Email Notifications

Approval, rejection, and account messages arrive at the email you registered with. Check spam if missing.

City Hall

For lost ID cards or technical issues, contact the LGU Borongan IT desk at City Hall.
